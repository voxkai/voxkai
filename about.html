who am i?

i am an electronic music producer from the DC area. i have been making music since 2015 and have been using FL studio since late 2016. the voxkai project started in may 2020 after putting my previous alias to an end.

as of october 2020, i have 4 singles and 1 remix released, and my debut album releasing soon.

i use they/them pronouns.
